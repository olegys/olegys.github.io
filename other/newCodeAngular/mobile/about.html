About us

ON-THE-GO platform is your personal assistant in the English language study. It is only you who choose the schedule, monitor your achievements and devote as much time to the lessons as you consider at any place anytime you like.

ON-THE-GO is created on the basis of ECTV Educational Channel. 8-year experience of successful work has been specially analyzed and used in new educational courses with dynamic tasks. Every taken lesson is easily implemented in your everyday life, so, your process of study presents not only learning the material but also immediate usage of your knowledge.

At present, only the full site version is available, that is why it may work incorrectly on your mobile devices. Join ON-THE-GO now using your PC and learn English comfortably and with pleasure!
